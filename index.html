Handaan Highlight

Well-loved dishes served in Filipino
celebrations

Letchon Baboy

Our crown jewel featuring ultra-crispy skin and succulent, flavorful roasted meat. No Filipino fiesta is ever truly complete without this centerpiece dish.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Crispy, golden spring rolls packed with savory minced meat. They are the ultimate party starter and a guaranteed crowd favorite.

Pancit Bihon

Stir-fried rice noodles tossed with fresh vegetables and savory bits of meat. This dish represents long life and is a staple at every celebration.

Mango Float

Layered Graham crackers, whipped cream, and sweet, ripe Manila mangoes. This chilled dessert is a refreshing and creamy tropical delight.

Halo-Halo

The ultimate Filipino cooler featuring shaved ice, creamy milk, and sweet toppings. It is a colorful explosion of textures and flavors in one glass.

Relapse Set

"Diet starts tomorrow!" The ultimate barkada sampler featuring your favorite Pika-Pika and deep-fried cravings. It is the perfect companion for late-night chika and broken resolutions with the whole squad.

Sharron Set

For the grand milestones where "balot" is mandatory! This massive feast includes our premium Lechon and a variety of signature mains to feed a crowd. It is big enough to celebrate the moment and still have enough for tomorrow’s lunch.

Hapunan Set

Bring the warmth of a home-cooked Filipino dinner directly to your family table. This set offers a balanced mix of comforting stews, rice, and refreshing drinks for a hassle-free evening. It is the perfect way to enjoy quality time over a delicious, hearty meal.
