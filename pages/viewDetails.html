Cultural Background

A staple in fiestas, weddings, birthdays, and family gatherings, Lechon Baboy has become one of the most iconic celebration dishes in the Philippines. Influenced by the Spanish colonial period, it grew into a symbol of abundance, hospitality, and shared tradition.
