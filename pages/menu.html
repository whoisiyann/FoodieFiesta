Main Course

Lechon Baboy

Our crown jewel featuring ultra-crispy skin and succulent, flavorful roasted meat. No Filipino fiesta is ever truly complete without this centerpiece dish.

Chicken Inasal

Bacolod-style grilled chicken marinated in lemongrass, ginger, and achiote oil. It is charred to perfection for a smoky and authentic regional taste.

Pork Barbecue

Tender pork slices on sticks, grilled with a thick and savory-sweet sauce. These skewers are a nostalgic staple for any festive gathering.

Beef Caldereta

Hearty beef stew simmered in a rich tomato sauce with peppers and cheese. It is a savory, melt-in-your-mouth classic that feels like a warm hug.

Pork Adobo

The iconic Filipino classic of pork braised in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. Its savory and tangy profile makes it a timeless favorite for everyone.

Kare-Kare

Tender meat and fresh vegetables stewed in a thick, creamy peanut sauce. Serve it with a side of bagoong for the ultimate flavor experience.

Sinigang na Baboy

A comforting, sour tamarind broth loaded with tender pork and garden vegetables. It is the perfect soul-warming dish for any time of day.

Pancit Bihon

Stir-fried rice noodles tossed with fresh vegetables and savory bits of meat. This dish represents long life and is a staple at every celebration.
